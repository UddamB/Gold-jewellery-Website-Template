How it works

A simple, respectful process from appraisal to release.

We focus on clarity. Customers bring in or submit details for their item, we assess metal content, condition, brand or collectible value where relevant, and then provide a clear loan offer with term details before anything moves ahead.

1. Appraisal and verification We inspect purity, weight, condition, and resale profile to determine a fair secured value.
2. Offer and documentation You receive loan terms, repayment expectations, and collateral handling details in plain language.
3. Funding and secure storage Approved loans can be funded quickly while pledged items remain documented and securely held.

Common questions

Important details before you book an appraisal.

What items do you accept?

Gold, silver, bullion, select coins, and jewellery with recoverable metal or resale value are commonly eligible.

How is the loan amount decided?

Offers are based on appraisal results, purity, weight, condition, market pricing, and any collectible premium where applicable.

Is approval guaranteed?

No. All loans are subject to appraisal, identity checks, and internal approval criteria.
